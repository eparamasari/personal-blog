Motivation

Motivate yourself.

What is motivation? How can we motivate ourselves?

Motivation is a tricky thing. According to Daniel Pink in his book, Drive, there are two kinds of motivation, extrinsic and intrinsic.

Extrinsic motivators are material motivators such as money, bonuses, prizes, etc.

Contrariwise, intrinsic motivators include the job itself. This can be because the work is fun, rewarding, or serving a greater purpose.

When learning how to code for the first time, it would make it less overwhelming when you see it as something fun in itself.
